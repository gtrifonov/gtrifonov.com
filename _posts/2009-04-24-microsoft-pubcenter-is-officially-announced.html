Yesterday Kevin McCabe officially announced launch of Microsoft PubCenter Beta (https://beta.pubcenter.microsoft.com/). I am happy to see news headlines because a lot of time and efforts has been contributed to make this happen. Hopefully our team will get a lot of feedback which will make product better and will provide more alternative to publishers who are using Google Ad Sense. To try product you need to be US based publisher and provide some information regarding your web site to be eligible.  The big advantage for big publishers that they don’t need to choose between PubCenter and other networks, you can simply rotate ad units from different networks to see what network works better for you. Also product provide great tools for ad unit look and feel customization and running experiments to see what adunit appearance works better for your pages. Try it and provide your feedback.
