As part of my blog optimization i decided to start sharing some SEO tips, as i will go ahead myself to migrate my blog from custom made solution to open source platform.

 

So here we go: SE0 TIP 1 – Make your site to be discoverable by crawlers

A lot of time I am seeing flying magic done in flash or  cool JavaScript tricks and effects. If you doing it with your site, make sure to have ‘old fashioned’ links so crawlers can discover your content, probably not a lot of users will see your brilliant design if your don’t have proper navigation defined in html.

None of optimization tricks will work if Google,Bing,Yahoo will not be able to discover your content existence.
